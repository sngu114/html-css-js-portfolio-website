    Programming Digital Media - Final Project Documentation

    -

    The player controls a hungry LSU campus squirrel trying to survive by collecting food scraps discarded in the trash by LSU students. As the player collects falling food from above, they must be careful about what they choose to eat, as some items can be detrimental to the squirrel’s health. Both food and trash will fall from above. The game ends when the player accidentally eats trash. The player has 3 lives, and as the difficulty increases, food falls faster, giving the player more chances to mess up.

    -

    - Hardware: 3 LEDs, Buzzer, Button, Joystick

    - Sound: Win Screen Sound, Lose Screen Sound, Player Eating Sound

    - Graphics: Welcome Screen, Game Screen, Win/Lose Screen, Squirrel + Win/Lose Sprites, Trash Bag, Chicken Nuggets, Coffee Cup

    -

    Materials used from kit:

    Arduino Elegoo Uno R3, 830 Tie Points Breadboard, 17x wires, 3x RGB LEDs, Buzzer(Active), Button, Joystick Module, 3x 220 Resistors, 1k Resistor, 10k Resistor, Laptop.

    -

    Programmed with: Visual Studio Code, Arduino IDE
